About

Hello, my name is Christopher, but you can call me CJ. I have a strong passion for web designing and web development. I enjoy the process of building websites from the ground up, with a strong intent of doing my best. Each new website I create is always better than the last, only getting better with time. I feel that many websites are poorly developed, many don’t have any support for smart phone screens, or “too complex” design for the elderly; websites should be a fast and smooth experience to get the information you need, not a maze to get to a small page. I offer a simple and easy process to make these simple, but well-designed websites. Coding with HTML, CSS and JavaScript feels very easy, simple, and has never held back any design I’ve had before. I offer many options and designs to give you the best simplest website you could ever need.
